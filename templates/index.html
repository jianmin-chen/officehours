How does it work?

It's as simple as it sounds: office hours for your inbox. Choose a time. People will only be able to message you during this time, and we'll notify your inbox if anybody messages you during these hours.

Create a group and invite some people to it! If you have Google Classroom, we can add all the members of a class automatically.
Set up some office hours. The people in your group will only be able to message you during these times, and you'll only be notified by us during these times.
Clear your email notifications by searching us! Boom, your email doesn't have messages you'll never answer anymore.
